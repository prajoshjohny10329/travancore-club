Travancore Club Thiruvalla

Where Tradition Meets Togetherness

Travancore Club, established in 1994 in Thiruvalla, Kerala, is more than just a social club — it is a community rooted in culture, camaraderie, and shared growth. What began as a dream among a few passionate individuals at Pandanpadavil has grown into one of Kerala’s most vibrant and respected socio-recreational institutions. The club was founded by a visionary group of members including Jacob Kattassery, Thomas Koshy, Dr. George Varghese, and others who laid the foundation for what would become a second home to over 1,000 members today. From its early days in a rented space, the club has flourished into a sprawling 6.5-acre campus, inaugurated with grace by Princess Aswathy Thirunal Gowri Lakshmi Bayi.

Affiliated Clubs Across India

Travancore Club is proudly affiliated with more than 72 prestigious clubs across India, offering our members extended access to facilities and privileges beyond Thiruvalla. This vast network ensures that wherever our members travel, they can enjoy the comfort, hospitality, and services of like-minded institutions.

From metropolitan cities to heritage towns, our affiliations reflect our commitment to expanding opportunities for cultural exchange, professional networking, and leisure travel. Whether it's a business trip, a family vacation, or a sporting event, our affiliated club network ensures you’re always at home—no matter where you are.

Mission

Travancore Club is committed to fostering a vibrant community through cultural, recreational, and social engagement. We aim to provide a welcoming space where members of all ages can connect, grow, and participate in activities that enrich both individual lives and the collective spirit of Thiruvalla.
