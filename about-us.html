About Travancore Club

Rooted in Tradition. Thriving in Community.

Travancore Club, established in 1994 in Thiruvalla, Kerala, is more than just a social club — it is a community rooted in culture, camaraderie, and shared growth. What began as a dream among a few passionate individuals at Pandanpadavil has grown into one of Kerala’s most vibrant and respected socio-recreational institutions.

The club was founded by a visionary group of members including Jacob Kattassery, Thomas Koshy, Dr. George Varghese, and others who laid the foundation for what would become a second home to over 1,000 members today. From its early days in a rented space, the club has flourished into a sprawling 6.5-acre campus, inaugurated with grace by Princess Aswathy Thirunal Gowri Lakshmi Bayi.

Over the years, Travancore Club has developed a wide range of modern amenities, including a spacious auditorium, floodlit tennis and shuttle courts, a serene walkway, card room, guest house, gymnasium, music hall, restaurant, and more — all crafted to support an active and enriching lifestyle for members of all ages.

Mission

Travancore Club is committed to fostering a vibrant community through cultural, recreational, and social engagement. We aim to provide a welcoming space where members of all ages can connect, grow, and participate in activities that enrich both individual lives and the collective spirit of Thiruvalla.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading socio-recreational institution in Kerala, rooted in tradition yet evolving with modern needs. We aspire to build a legacy of inclusivity, excellence, and social responsibility—serving as a model community hub that nurtures personal development, civic values, and lifelong friendships.

Our Role in the Community

Travancore Club is a platform for social engagement, youth development, and civic responsibility. Through cultural events, sporting competitions, government partnerships, and charitable activities, the club remains committed to giving back to society.

Membership at a Glance

Becoming a member of Travancore Club means joining a network of individuals who value quality, community, and cultural connection. Members enjoy access to all facilities, exclusive events, and affiliations with over 72 reputed clubs across India — extending privileges far beyond Thiruvalla.
